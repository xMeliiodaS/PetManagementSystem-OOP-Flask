Welcome to Pet Management System

This app helps you manage your pets efficiently.

Features:

  • Add new pets with details such as name, species, age, owner, and vaccination status.
  • Track the total number of pets in the system.
  • Manage different types of pets, including dogs with specific breeds.
  • View detailed information about each pet.

Get started by adding your pets and keeping their information up to date!
